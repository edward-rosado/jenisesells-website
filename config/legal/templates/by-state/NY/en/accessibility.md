## New York City Human Rights Law

The NYCHRL provides broad disability protections, including the requirement that places of public accommodation — including businesses operating websites accessible to New York City residents — make reasonable accommodations for people with disabilities.

If you require an accommodation to access information on this website or to use our real estate services, we will make reasonable efforts to provide one. Please contact us using the information below.

## Measures We Take

To support accessibility, we take the following measures:
- Include accessibility as part of our website design and content decisions
- Use semantic HTML to convey structure and meaning
- Provide text alternatives for non-text content
- Ensure sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds
- Make all functionality available via keyboard navigation
- Avoid content that could trigger seizures or physical reactions
- Provide clear and consistent navigation
