We believe exceptional service means being accessible to everyone. Sterling & Associates is committed to ensuring that our website and digital presence are fully accessible to individuals with disabilities, in accordance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

We continually review and improve our digital experience to remove barriers and provide an equal experience for all visitors. If you encounter any accessibility issues or require assistance accessing any portion of our site, please contact us directly and we will respond promptly.

Your ability to access our services should never be limited by a disability. We welcome your feedback as we work toward a more inclusive digital presence.
